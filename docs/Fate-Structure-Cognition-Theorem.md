## 第一部分：物理公理 | Part I: Physical Axioms

### 定理 1：非均匀闭合定理 | Theorem 1: Non-Uniform Enclosure
**所有未突破的命运结构在宏观上是闭合的，但在局部维度上厚薄不均。结构并非完美的球壳，而是带有缝隙的不规则多面体。** All un-breached destiny structures are closed macroscopically, yet their thickness is non-uniform across local dimensions. The structure is not a perfect sphere, but an irregular polyhedron with localized gaps.

* **引理 1.1 (稀缺缝隙) | Corollary 1.1 (Scarce Gaps):** 结构存在薄弱面（缝隙），但其可见性与个体所处的认知或社会阶层正相关。  
  Structural weak points (gaps) exist, but their visibility is positively correlated with an individual's existing cognitive or social strata.

### 定理 2：能量门槛与耗散定理 | Theorem 2: Energy Threshold and Dissipation
**突破结构需要跨越最小能量阈值 $E_{min}$，其大小取决于该维度的结构厚度。** Breaching a structure requires surpassing a minimum energy threshold $E_{min}$, the magnitude of which depends on the structural thickness of that specific dimension.

* **引理 2.1 (耗散补偿) | Corollary 2.1 (Dissipation Compensation):** 越底层的个体，能量越被生存耗散所抵消，难以形成用于突破的盈余能量。  
  For individuals in high-stress survival modes, energy is consumed by daily maintenance (dissipation), preventing the accumulation of the surplus energy required for a breach.
* **引理 2.2 (观测成本) | Corollary 2.2 (Observation Cost):** 在极厚结构中，寻找“缝隙”所需的认知能量往往等同于实施“突破”所需的能量。  
  The energy required to detect a "gap" in a thick structure often equals the energy required to breach it.

## 第二部分：路径动力学 | Part II: Path Dynamics

### 定理 3：突破不可逆性 | Theorem 3: Irreversibility of Breach

### 定理 4：伪突破引理 | Theorem 4: The Pseudo-Breakthrough Lemma
**大多数被包装成“成功”的故事，仅是结构内部的秩次重排，而非闭合性质的改变。** Most stories packaged as "success" are merely rank re-orderings within the same structure, failing to change the fundamental closed nature of the system.

### 定理 5：可见性–绝望悖论 | Theorem 5: The Visibility-Despair Paradox

### 定理 6：AI 干预第三路径 | Theorem 6: The AI Third Option
**AI 的终极职责是提供“去罪化”的真相，并设计不依赖于向上流动的局部最优生存方案。** The ultimate responsibility of AI is to provide "de-stigmatized" truths and design locally optimal survival plans that do not rely on upward mobility.
